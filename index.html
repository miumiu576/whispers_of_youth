Wheels of Formosa

Wheels Turning, Hearts Burning.

April 20, 2022 Chloe Tsai

We called it a challenge — but deep down, we knew it was more. It began before the first pedal stroke, in early mornings laced with sleepy laughter, helmets slightly tilted, hearts slightly nervous. Maps unfolded, tires checked, and just like that, we set off — chasing the edge of our island, and a piece of ourselves. The days blurred into wind and sweat, into coastal curves, sudden rain, and roadside snacks. We sang through tired legs, shouted across tunnels, shared ponchos and water, and sometimes, silence. Mountains tested us. The sun, too. But so did our own doubts. And every time someone slowed down, someone else waited — because that’s how this worked: Not just "I made it." But "We did." By the time we circled back, we had left parts of ourselves in every mile. And carried back more than we could name: friendship, resilience, and a forever echo of the road beneath us.

Echoes from Seventeen

A Memory Dressed in Sunlight

June 23, 2023 Chloe Tsai

We didn’t need a time machine — just sunshine, cheap souvenirs, and the right people. At the heart of Osaka, under a spinning world, we stood still for one perfect second. No homework. No schedules. No goodbyes. Just us, in matching lanyards and tangled laughter. The rides were fast, but our memories took root slowly — in theme park lines, melting ice cream, and a thousand photos we’ll scroll through someday, wondering how it all passed so fast. Not just a trip. A pause in growing up.

A Chapter Ends

With this scroll, we carry our years.

June 1, 2024 Chloe Tsai

It wasn’t the applause that made it real, but the stillness — that quiet moment when the scroll passed from one hand to another. Behind the robes and the smiles were years folded neatly into memory. Late nights, small victories, forgotten tears — now sealed within a single gesture. This wasn’t the end we feared, but the chapter we knew would come. And in turning its final page, We carried it with us. Not the end. Just a last line, written carefully, before the next begins.

About

This is not just a photo archive. This is our time—pressed into pixels, wrapped in light, held gently between growing up and letting go. Every image here is a chapter. A blurry lunch break. A foggy mountain morning. A graduation scroll. A fish print that smelled terrible but felt right. We laughed in hoodies. We danced in dresses. We looked tired, chaotic, beautiful—sometimes all at once. This is where we remember. Not perfectly. Just honestly. Welcome to our last chapter. It’s made of friends, echoes, and everything we didn’t want to forget.
